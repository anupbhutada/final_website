About

EXTROPY 2K18 is the second edition of the annual extravaganza where students from all disciplines can come together and experience the enthralling world of chemical engineers. We have invited-lectures, workshops, and a wide range of events, exhibitions, and competitions to be held during it along with research paper presentations. It provides an opportunity to all the budding Chemical Engineers to compete, exchange ideas, collaborate and learn and ofcourse have fun all under the same roof.

NIVAARAN

Awaken the nature lover in you and get ready to solve environmental problems that affect us all. Design feasible prototypes and come up with green solutions. Strategize your idea judiciously and crack the code to a better future.

Problem statements relating to various environment problems to be given to participants.
The first round is abstract submission on the problem.
Second round needs to be a prototype video submission tackling the problem.
Final round comprises of prototype and report presentation which will be judged by the professors.

INDUSTRY101!

When all theoretical knowledge falters in the face of an industrial calamity, one needs mental fortitude and resourcefulness to counter crises. Find out if you can bridge the gap between industry and institutional learning to handle some of the fundamental challenges commonly found in industries.

Problem statements relating to industrial calamities to be provided on day 1 of the fest.
Presentation by participants tackling these problems on day 2 of the fest to be adjudged by the professors.

ASPEN WORKSHOP

ASPEN PLUS is the market-leading chemical process optimization software used by chemical, biochemical as well as polymer industries. Used for designing, operation and optimization of safe and profitable manufacturing facilities, it finds its application in various fields of chemical engineering making its knowledge an absolute must for all aspiring to enter this field. Be a part of this enlightening workshop to further your understanding of chemical engineering.

PotionMasters

Ever felt you needed a place to make your own concoctions. Come and get a chance to make your own blends and tickle the taste buds of the crowd with your culinary artistry.

Unknown edible liquids to be given to participants to form a blend.
Decide on a composition and make the mixture.
If the crowd loves the flavour of your mix, you win.

CHEMHUNT

Let your inquisitive nature take the lead and guide you through a journey to decipher the codes and puzzles laid out for you. Figure out the clues, solve the mystery and move forward in search of that elusive treasure.

No limit on team size.
Clues to be solved to decipher the puzzle while exploring the whole campus during the hunt.

POSEIDON

Ever thought what you would do if you could walk on water. Feel the grandeur of Gods, or act like a ninja while you take a stroll on water. Get a thrilling experience and enjoy the grandeur while you get a chance.

PsyChE

Dont know engineering? No one knows actually. Fool others to make them think you know stuff! Make up witty answers to sensible questions you dont know the response to? With a chemical engineering twist in this fun game, convince others well enough and you might just win the ultimate prize.

Played in groups of 6-8.
Admin gives a question for which every player submits an answer.
The players then each choose an answer from the submitted lot.
If your answer gets selected by maximum number of people, you win the game.
